About Me

I am Beckham Kunda, an Information Technology student at North Seattle College focused on networking and systems administration. I am building a foundation in web development, computer systems, and technical problem-solving so I can support reliable digital infrastructure.

My background started in Zambia, where I developed a strong interest in computers, business, and how technology can connect people to better opportunities. That background motivates me to keep learning practical skills that can be used in real workplaces and communities.

Through my coursework, I am gaining hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, GitHub, responsive design, and basic troubleshooting. I enjoy learning how websites are structured, how code changes affect the user experience, and how projects can be organized professionally.

One accomplishment I am proud of is continuing to improve my web projects by testing, revising, and publishing them through GitHub Pages. Each assignment helps me become more comfortable with version control, clean file organization, and explaining my work clearly.

My career goal is to grow into a networking or systems administration role where I can help configure, maintain, and protect the technology that people depend on every day. I want my portfolio to show that I am reliable, curious, and serious about developing real technical skill.

Outside of class, I enjoy exploring technology ideas, learning about computer hardware, and imagining future projects that combine networking, web design, and entrepreneurship. To see my latest work, visit my Projects page or review my code on GitHub.
